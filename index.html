Manuni Inn Dharamsala

OVERVIEW

Elegant and Secluded

Boutique Hotel on the Manuni Riverside.
At an altitude of 1315 M above sea level at Dharamshala, Manuni Inn offers a sublime slice of elegant 21 rooms on the Himalayan foothills, with an emphasis on elegant simplicity. Manuni Inn is located right in the lap of the picturesque steep Dhauladhar Mountains, in the enchanting village of Khanyara, Dharamshala.
The accommodation at the Hotel Manuni Inn is perfect for everyone wanting to enjoy nature in peace and comfort in their own leisure. The hotel rooms are built in style with all Western comforts, reminding you of casual and roomy ambience. Our guests are always surrounded by the beauty of the region as panoramic windows have been installed in all our rooms for spectacular natural views.
