Welness

Because It’s the Extras that Count.

We’ve got everything you need to keep you feeling good, whether you want to work out, wind down or simply want some help weighing up what to do with your time on the Amalfi Coast. Casa Angelina guests have access to state-of-the-art facilities and services, from a well-equipped wellness center to our indoor and outdoor swimming pools to the barefoot-luxe La Gavitella Beach Club. Guests will also have access to our clued-in concierge who can help arrange tours of all kind, including private excursions on a boat reserved exclusively for your use.
